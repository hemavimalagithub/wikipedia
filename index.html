WIKIPEDIA

Wikipedia is a free and open online encyclopedia that has revolutionized the way people access and share knowledge on the internet. Launched in January 2001, Wikipedia has since become one of the largest and most popular reference sources in the world.

WIKIPEDIANS

Wikipedians are the passionate and dedicated volunteers who contribute to Wikipedia, the world's largest and most widely used online encyclopedia. These individuals are the driving force behind the creation, maintenance, and expansion of Wikipedia's vast repository of knowledge.

Volunteerism: The overwhelming majority of Wikipedians are volunteers who dedicate their time and expertise to Wikipedia without any monetary compensation. Their motivation is fueled by a sense of purpose, a commitment to the principles of free knowledge, and a desire to make information accessible to people around the world.
